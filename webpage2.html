Health & Eligibility

who eligible for blood donation



General Guidelines. To donate blood or platelets, you must be in good general health, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be at least 16 years old. Parental consent is required for blood donation by 16 year olds; 16 year olds are NOT eligible to donate platelets.

What are the conditions for blood donation?



Any donor, who is healthy, fit and not suffering from any transmittable diseases can donate blood. Donor must be 18 -60 years age and having a minimum weight of 50Kg can donate blood. A donor can again donate blood after 3 months of your last donation of blood.
